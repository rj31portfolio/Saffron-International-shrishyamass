Controllers

Controller

Integrated controller systems for high-speed wire forming, spring coiling, and multi-axis automation.

High-performance control for demanding production

Our controller lineup is engineered for stable multi-axis synchronization, fast cycle times, and dependable operation in high-temperature, high-dust industrial environments. Flexible I/O, rich communication interfaces, and a modern HMI platform enable accurate motion control, fast setup, and remote monitoring for wire forming and spring manufacturing lines.

Control & Connectivity
  • Adaptive multi-axis synchronization with speed priority control.
