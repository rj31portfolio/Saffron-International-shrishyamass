SFI-XS 80BC / SFI-XS 80BC-S

Compact design for a wide variety of roller handle shapes with fast changeover.

SFI-XS 80BC SFI-XS 80BC-S

Versatile roller handle production

  • Designed for many kinds of roller handles without a mold.
  • Produces hundreds of different angle shapes with a compact footprint.
  • Servo motor shaping with programmable control.
  • Punching and ear stamping controlled by hydraulic cylinders.
  • Fast changeover reduces cost and supports 90-degree handle demand.
  • Feeding length range: 100-650 mm.
